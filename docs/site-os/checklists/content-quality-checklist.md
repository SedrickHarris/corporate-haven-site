# Content Quality Checklist

Use this checklist to confirm a page's body copy is optimized, conversion-focused, and free of fake claims. Pairs with `seo-aeo-qa-prompt.md` for the AEO-specific dimensions.

## 1. Not Thin

- [ ] At least 250 words of substantive body content beyond boilerplate
- [ ] Multiple H2 sections with real content (not just headings)
- [ ] Each section has a clear purpose (define, scope, who, why, how, FAQ, CTA)

## 2. Not Generic

- [ ] No "thorough cleaning solutions" / "industry-leading service" / "world-class quality" filler
- [ ] No paragraphs that could be lifted unchanged onto a competitor's page
- [ ] No literal city-name swaps from sibling pages
- [ ] Concrete specifics: what's wiped, vacuumed, sanitized; what surfaces are touched; what's included; what's not

## 3. Direct Answers

- [ ] Page answers its primary intent in the first 100 words
- [ ] Opening paragraph follows the 2–3 sentence direct-answer format
- [ ] Sentence 1: business + service + location
- [ ] No bait-and-switch (the intro promises something the body doesn't deliver)

## 4. Customer Pain Points

- [ ] At least 2 customer pain points addressed in body or FAQ
- [ ] Pain points are real (move-out deposit pressure, post-construction dust, scheduling around tenants, etc.)
- [ ] Each pain point is paired with a clear response from Corporate Haven's positioning

## 5. Service / Location Context

- [ ] Service scope explained in concrete terms
- [ ] Location context (if applicable) references real, publicly known characteristics
- [ ] Customer segments named ("families moving in", "office managers", "contractors finishing new builds")

## 6. Helpful FAQs

- [ ] 4–6 FAQs minimum (5–8 for high-value pages)
- [ ] Each Q is a real customer question
- [ ] Each A is 40–80 words
- [ ] Each A follows the direct-answer format
- [ ] At least 1 FAQ is voice-search friendly
- [ ] At least 1 FAQ is unique to this page (not a literal swap from a sibling)

## 7. Conversion CTA

- [ ] Primary CTA visible above the fold
- [ ] Primary CTA label is action-led ("Request a Free Quote", not "Contact us")
- [ ] Tap-to-call uses `tel:<PHONE — TODO-PHASE-F-LAUNCH>`
- [ ] Phone fallback near the form
- [ ] Final CTA section restates the offer + next step

## 8. No Keyword Stuffing

- [ ] Primary keyword appears 2–4× in body, naturally distributed
- [ ] Secondary keywords 1–2× each
- [ ] No paragraph with 3+ consecutive keyword repeats
- [ ] No H1 + H2 + H3 stacking the same exact phrase
- [ ] No hidden text

## 9. No Unsupported Claims

- [ ] No invented reviews / ratings / testimonials
- [ ] No invented license numbers / insurance details
- [ ] No invented years-in-business / jobs completed
- [ ] No invented certifications / awards / partnerships
- [ ] No invented same-day / emergency / 24/7 availability
- [ ] No pricing claims unless owner-confirmed
- [ ] No customer / team / project / before-after photos unless owner-supplied
- [ ] All TODO-VERIFY comments still in place where claims would otherwise live

## 10. Voice and Tone

- [ ] Professional, warm, family-owned voice (not corporate-stiff)
- [ ] Confident but not hype-filled
- [ ] No exclamation overload
- [ ] No "world-class", "industry-leading", "best of"
- [ ] Concrete over abstract
- [ ] Local-first phrasing where natural

## 11. Final Decision

- [ ] **Ship-ready** — content is optimized, conversion-focused, no fake claims
- [ ] **Strengthen** — run `content-strengthening-prompt.md` on specific gaps
- [ ] **Rewrite section** — one or more sections are thin or generic
- [ ] **STOP-SHIP** — invented data detected
